SECUREVAULT LTD. — FINANCIAL SUMMARY STATEMENT
Fiscal Year Ended 31 March 2024
Prepared for: Third-Party Vendor Due Diligence Review
Confidential — Not for Distribution

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

SecureVault Ltd. delivered stable financial performance in FY 2024 (year ended
31 March 2024). Total revenue grew 9.2% year-over-year to £8.7 million, supported
by continued subscription growth in the legal and financial services verticals.
EBITDA improved modestly to £1.2 million. The company carries moderate leverage
following a 2022 acquisition of a smaller competitor, though debt servicing remains
comfortably covered by operating cash flow. Management considers the current
financial position sound and the business well-positioned for continued growth.

6. NOTES ON LEVERAGE

The term loan was drawn in October 2022 to part-fund the acquisition of DocSafe
Technologies Ltd. The loan has a 5-year term with quarterly amortisation. The
original principal was £4.0 million; £0.8 million was repaid in FY 2024, consistent
with the repayment schedule. The company is in compliance with all financial
covenants as at 31 March 2024. Management expects the debt/equity ratio to normalise
to below 3.0x within the next two financial years as the loan amortises.
