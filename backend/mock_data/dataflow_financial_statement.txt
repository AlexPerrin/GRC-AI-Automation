DATAFLOW INC. — FINANCIAL SUMMARY STATEMENT
Fiscal Year Ended 31 December 2024
Prepared for: Third-Party Vendor Due Diligence Review
Confidential — Not for Distribution

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

DataFlow Inc. experienced a challenging fiscal year in FY 2024. Total revenue
declined by 12.4% year-over-year to $18.3 million, driven by elevated customer
churn in the mid-market segment and pricing pressure from larger incumbents.
The company recorded negative EBITDA of $(2.1) million and a net loss of
$(3.4) million. Cash reserves declined materially during the year. The company
undertook a restructuring in Q3 2024, reducing headcount by 22%.

6. MANAGEMENT COMMENTARY

Management acknowledges the difficult trading environment in FY 2024 and has
initiated a strategic review exploring options including additional equity financing,
a partial asset sale, or a strategic merger. The company is in early-stage
discussions with two potential investors. No binding agreements have been reached.

Going concern: The auditors have included an emphasis-of-matter paragraph in the
audit report regarding going concern uncertainty, given the covenant breach, negative
operating cash flow, and projected cash runway of less than 12 months without
additional financing.
