1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Acme Analytics Inc. delivered strong financial performance in FY 2024, achieving
£12.0 million in total revenue, representing 28% year-over-year growth driven by
expansion within the enterprise segment and international market entry. The company
reached EBITDA profitability in Q3 2024 and recorded positive net income for the
full year for the first time in its operating history.
